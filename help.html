Help Center

I deposited but the coins didn't reflect on my account
Make sure you return to the website after sending payment. Once verified by the bank, coins will automatically appear. If coins don’t appear after 5 minutes, contact customer support with proof of payment.
I registered my 1Win ID but still cannot access games
Ensure your ID is newly created and submitted correctly. Wait for approval confirmation and refresh the page. Contact support if issues persist.
My 1Win ID submission was rejected
Only newly created IDs are allowed. Older or inactive IDs are rejected. Create a new 1Win account and submit it again.
I cannot see the games or buttons are disabled
Your account may not be approved yet. Submit a valid 1Win ID and wait for confirmation. Refresh the page after approval to unlock games.
I accidentally closed the page during deposit
Coins will still be credited after bank verification. Return to the main page or contact support with proof if coins don’t appear after 5 minutes.
How do I contact customer support?
Click the 'Customer Support' button on the main page to open Telegram links. Send your issue clearly with proof if needed.
Can I play without registering a 1Win ID?
No. You must register and submit a valid 1Win ID to access all games and coins.
My coin balance decreased unexpectedly
Each prediction costs 1 coin. Ensure you check your prediction history. If discrepancies occur, contact support with your account ID.
I made multiple deposits at once, some coins are missing
Coins are added after each payment is verified individually. Wait a few minutes for each transaction. If coins are missing after 10 minutes, contact support.
I received an error when submitting my 1Win ID
Ensure your ID contains only numbers and is a newly created 1Win account. Refresh the page and try again. Contact support if the problem persists.
My game page shows "Server Unavailable"
Some games may be temporarily offline. Try again after a few minutes. If the issue continues, contact support.
How do I purchase coins?
To purchase coins, go to the main page and locate the “+” button next to your DM Coins balance. Click on it, and you will be directed to the Deposit Page. Complete your payment following the instructions provided. Once your payment is verified, the coins will automatically be added to your account balance.
I lost access to my 1Win account, what should I do?
You must create a new 1Win ID and submit it to continue using the website. Contact support for guidance if you still have issues.
I think there is a bug on the website
Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, send a detailed report to customer support.
